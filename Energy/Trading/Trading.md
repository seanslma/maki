## Purpose
Trading is typically said to serve three main purposes:
- **Arbitrage**: Taking advantage of price differences for the same or related assets across markets or time.
   - Goal: Risk-free or low-risk profit.
- **Speculation**: Taking positions based on expectations about future price movements.
   - Goal: Profit from price changes.
   - Involves risk.
- **Hedging**: Reducing or managing risk by offsetting exposures.
   - Goal: Protect against adverse price movements, not necessarily profit.
